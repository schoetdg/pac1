Reform Intiatives

Overview

The government has broken down the reforms its pursing into reform initiatives with milestones. You will find a detailed list of reform initiatives and the status of each in the Reform Initiative Milestones section. The Recent Key Highlights section features developments from the last few months that are progressing government reforms. The Historical Reports section provides information on vetting reform from previous quarters.



Recent Key Highlights

  1. Interagency began a series of workshops for prioritizing NBIB investigative backlog reductions recommendations.  In August, representatives from DoD, ODNI, OPM, NBIB, and the PAC PMO began meeting to review a number of proposed changes to reduce NBIB’s investigation backlog. These workshops, which are expected to conclude in October, will lead to a number of the recommendations being approved by the Executive Agents for implementation.
  2. ODNI Continuous Evaluation System cleared a review hurdle.  Continuous Evaluation (CE) is an automated capability to determine whether already cleared personnel have adverse information reported against them through repeated database checks. The ODNI’s CE system passed an Operational Readiness Review in August, which allows the agency to offer the capability the broader personnel vetting community.
  3. Shared Services for performing adjudications becomes available to NBIB customer agencies. In September, NBIB deployed an automated adjudication determination capability for cases where no adverse information was identified during a Tier 1 and Tier 3 investigation. This advancement will permit NBIB customers, which make up about 95% of Executive Branch agencies, to avoid the time and cost associated with a human adjudicating these cases. The Government hopes to expand this capability in the future.

Reform Initiative Milestones

The PAC’s vision for the future state falls into four primary Work Streams: Trusted Workforce, Modern Vetting, Secure and Modern Mission-Capable IT, and Continuous Performance Improvement.


Trusted Workforce

We must equip our workforce with the necessary training and resources to assist the workforce in responsibly reporting and/or self-reporting information of potential concern.

Modern Vetting

We must modernize our SSC vetting policies, processes, and workforce to reduce waste, increase quality, enhance effectiveness, and improve efficiency.

Secure and Modern Mission-Capable IT

We must secure the end-to-end environment, enhance SSC IT systems, establish SSC end-to-end shared services, and treat SSC data as a shared asset to support aligned, modern, and secure SSC IT systems and data.

Continuous Performance Improvement

We must establish a continuous performance improvement model and institutionalize outcome-based performance metrics to identify and drive enterprise-level enhancements to policy, oversight, and operational processes.
